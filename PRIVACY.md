# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 23, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how LensGeek ("the App") handles your information.

## Summary

LensGeek does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device.

## Camera and Microphone

LensGeek uses your device's camera and microphone to provide its real-time lens effects. All image and audio processing happens entirely on your device in real time. The App does not record, save, upload, or transmit any camera or microphone data to us or to any third party.

## Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App contains:

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No crash-reporting or usage-data collection
- No third-party SDKs that gather data

Any photos or videos you choose to save are stored only on your own device, in your Photos library, under your control. We never have access to them.

## Children's Privacy

LensGeek does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

## Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please [open an issue](https://github.com/minimaltek/LensGeek/issues) in this repository.

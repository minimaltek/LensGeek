# LensGeek

Surprise kids. Make adults laugh.

LensGeek is a collection of tricky real-time camera lenses for iOS, built around the themes of time and video.

## Lenses

- **FUNHOUSE MIRROR** - Distort your reflection like a carnival mirror
- **MANDALA** - Transform your camera into a kaleidoscope
- **TILT SHIFT** - Make the real world look like a tiny miniature
- **CHRONO** - Leave Marey-style motion trails as you move
- **SLIT SCAN** - Bend time with a classic slit-scan effect
- **SPEECH** - AI narrates what it sees through your lens
- **CUTUP** - Beat-synced audio and video cutup
- **MARBLE** - Fluid marble-like video distortion
- **IDENTITY ZOOM** - Zoom into the moment

Some lenses are still works in progress. More updates and new lenses coming soon.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: The app won't open or crashes on startup**  
A: Make sure you have iOS 17.0 or later. If the problem persists, try deleting and reinstalling the app.

**Q: I can't see the camera preview**  
A: Please grant camera permission. Go to Settings > LensGeek > Camera and enable access.

**Q: How do I save photos and videos?**  
A: Tap the SHOT button for photos or the REC button to start/stop video recording. Your creations are saved to your Camera Roll.

**Q: The app is asking for microphone access. Why?**  
A: Microphone access is needed to record audio with your videos. You can deny this if you only want silent videos.

**Q: Effects look different in landscape vs portrait mode**  
A: This is by design! Some effects adapt to screen orientation for the best visual experience.

**Q: How do I switch between lenses?**  
A: Tap the lens icons on the right side of the screen to switch between different effects.

## Privacy

LensGeek respects your privacy:

- All effects are processed locally on your device
- No personal data is collected or transmitted
- Camera/microphone are used only for real-time effects and recording
- Photos and videos are saved only to your device
